Duties

Pilots have full control and responsibility for the safety of the passengers and crew members, operation of the aircraft, and the flight decisions made once in air. Some duties of a pilot are:

  • Perform pre-flight checklist on the aircraftâ€™s parts (engine, hydraulics, etc.)
  • Communicate with crew members and perform crosschecks
  • Check weather conditions and react to environmental changes and altitude
  • Create a flight plan, detailing the route of the flight, the altitude necessary, and amount of fuel required

Required Skills

Pilots require specific skills such as:

  • Active Listening (giving full attention to crew members)
  • Operation and Control (controlling operations of equipment and systems such as the aircraft)
  • Monitoring (watching gauges, dials, and other indicators to make sure the aircraft is suitable to fly)
  • Critical Thinking (ability to think quickly if a problem occurs on flight route)
