Peak Airlines Airport Operations

If you enjoy working in a fast-paced environment where no two days are the same, consider a career in Airport Operations where you will interact with our guests or support their travel experience.

Apply Now

Duties

Airport operations maintain the entire airfield area of an airport. They are responsible for ensuring the safe take-off and landing of an aircraft. Some of their duties include:

  • Coordinating between air-traffic control and maintenance personnel
  • Implementing airfield safety procedures
  • Monitoring and maintaining flight records
  • Using airfield landing and navigational aids

Required Skills

  • Speaking (effectively communication to others)
  • Operation and Control (controlling operations of equipment and systems such as cart vehicles)
  • Management of Personnel Resources (motivating, developing, and directing people as they work)
  • Systems Analysis (determining how a system should work and how changes in conditions, operations, and the environments affects the work environment)
