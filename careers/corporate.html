Alliances

The Alliances team works to provide our customers with seamless travel around the world through agreements between Peak and other airlines.

Community Affairs

Our Community Affairs co-workers manage our corporate giving, nonprofit partnerships and volunteer programs.

Continuous Improvement and Enterprise Optimization

CIEO focuses on how we can advance the quality and efficiency of what we do across our operations and around the world.

Corporate Communications

Our Corporate Communications department manages relationships with the media and shares information about the company both externally and to our employees worldwide.

Corporate and Government Affairs

The Corporate and Government Affairs team works closely with the U.S. government, as well as coordinating grassroots advocacy efforts, to represent Peak on public policy and legislative issues.

eCommerce

The eCommerce team works to create best-in-class customer experiences across our digital channels, including flypeak.com, the Peak app and more.

Environmental Affairs

Help Peak take actions today to shape an environmentally sustainable future.

Ethics and Compliance

Our Ethics and Compliance Office coordinates compliance programs at Peak and manages the company's ethics platform.

Finance

Explore open positions in our Finance department, which manages the company's budgets, forecasts and investments, and provides analysis of our financial performance and strategies.

Human Resources

The Human Resources team helps attract and recruit diverse talent, and works to provide employees with competitive benefits, leadership resources and the support to be successful at Peak.

Information Technology

The Peak IT team designs, develops and maintains massively scaling technology solutions that are brought to life with innovative architectures, data analytics and digital solutions

Internal Audit

Internal auditors have the opportunity to look at our organization objectively, and provide feedback on areas for improvement.

Legal

From airports to the boardroom, our Legal department provides advice and counsel on all aspects of the business, assessing legal risks and our compliance.

Marketing

The Marketing department works to ensure that the Peak experience, products and loyalty program are the best in the airline industry, and communicates these unique features to our millions of customers.

Pricing and Revenue Management

The Pricing and Revenue Management department helps us run a successful business by predicting consumer demand and setting up pricing structures.

Sales

Join the largest revenue-generating department at Peak. Our Sales teams work closely with travel agencies, Peak travel departments and corporate clients.
