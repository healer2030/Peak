About PeakPoints

PeakPoints is our reward program for our loyal customers.

As a PeakPoints Member, you earn points by flying and can use them how and when you want. Plus, if you’re short on points, you can always purchase more to get that reward you’ve been eyeing.

Apply online through our application form and we'll be in touch with you shortly

PeakPoints Benefits

Priority Boarding

Be one of the first to board with the best available boarding pass.

Checked Bags

Get two checked bags free when you book a flight using your PeakPoints.

Discounted Purchases

Get 20% off on inflight purchases using your PeakPoints credit card.

Card

Earn more points with our PeakPoints credit card, which you get free when you join PeakPoints.

Merchandise

Using you PeakPoints to buy Merchandise will earn you more PeakPoints when you advertise our merchandise on social networks.

Redeem points

Use your points to land great one-way and roundtrip award flights to your favorite Peak destinations–with absolutely no blackout dates!

While redemption levels vary, the amount of points a certain trip requires is directly tied to our current fares, and when flight fares are lower, so are award flight point fares. When searching for your next travel adventure, pay with your PeakPoints card, and select "Pay With Points" at the check out.
